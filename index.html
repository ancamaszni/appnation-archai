How we calculate your savings

App / Play Store

The App / Play Store charge 30% commission to act as an ‘MoR’. They also charge fixed fees for refunds/chargebacks and take FX spread on payouts – we assume this is ~4% of GMV.

Indirect taxes

For Mexico you pay the same IVA with app / play store and with Outpost, so there is no difference. In Brazil, we set up a structure that means no extra tax - otherwise, this would climb to >50% sales tax!

Outpost fee

Outpost is a flat 8.5% of GMV with no extra lines. The calculator compares that against Store and Direct totals to show your savings.
