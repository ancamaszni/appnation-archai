How we calculate your savings

App / Play Store

The App / Play Store charge a 32% total fee to act as a Merchant of Record (MoR). This includes the 30% commission plus additional costs for refunds and chargebacks.

Outpost fee

Outpost is a flat percentage of GMV. We do not cover refunds / chargeback fees - but negotiate the lowest on the market for you. We compare that to the Store total to show net value unlocked.
